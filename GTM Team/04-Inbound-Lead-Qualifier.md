# 04 — Inbound Lead Qualifier → HubSpot + Slack


## What it does
Triggers on Typeform/website form submit, enriches the lead (Clearbit), scores fit + intent via GPT against your ICP rubric, creates/updates HubSpot contact, and pings the right AE in Slack for hot leads.

## Why it wins for GTM
Routes hot leads to an AE in under 60 seconds — fastest measurable ROI win.

## Stack
Typeform / Webhook → Clearbit → OpenAI (scoring) → HubSpot → Slack.

## Setup notes
- Publish the rubric so reps trust the score.
- Define "hot" threshold with sales leadership.
- Log every score for monthly calibration.
