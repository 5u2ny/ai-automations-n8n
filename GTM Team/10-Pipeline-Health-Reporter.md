# 10 — Pipeline Health Reporter → Weekly Slack Digest


## What it does
Queries CRM each Monday for stage movement, stalled deals, and forecast deltas; GPT writes a narrative "state of pipeline" memo for founder/board updates.

## Why it wins for GTM
Replaces the weekly pipeline meeting prep; gives the founder board-ready commentary on demand.

## Stack
HubSpot/Salesforce → OpenAI → Slack + Notion archive.

## Setup notes
- Compare against last week and last month deltas.
- Call out specific deal names, not just totals.
- Archive each memo to Notion for trend review.
