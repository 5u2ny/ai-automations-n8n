# 03 — LinkedIn Founder-Led Content Agent


## What it does
Takes raw voice notes or bullet points from the founder, uses GPT to expand into a polished LinkedIn post matching their voice, queues to a scheduler.

## Why it wins for GTM
Founder-led content is the highest-ROI brand channel for early-stage AI startups.

## Stack
Telegram/Voice memo upload → Whisper transcription → OpenAI → Buffer / Typefully / direct LinkedIn API.

## Setup notes
- Feed the agent 10+ past posts as voice samples.
- Always human-review before publish.
- Track impressions back to a Sheet for learning.
