# 04 — Meeting Transcription → Action Items → Notion/Asana


## What it does
Pulls call transcripts from Fireflies/Otter, GPT extracts decisions and action items with owners + due dates, pushes tasks into Notion / Asana / Linear.

## Why it's a safe first AI win
Universal pain killer; every team feels the value on day one.

## Stack
Fireflies/Otter → OpenAI → Notion / Asana / Linear.

## Setup notes
- Match owners by email against the project tool.
- Default due date to +7 days if not stated.
- Send a Slack summary to attendees within 5 min.
